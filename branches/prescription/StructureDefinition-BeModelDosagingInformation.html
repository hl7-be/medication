Logical Model: DosagingInformation

Official URL: https://www.ehealth.fgov.be/standards/fhir/medication/StructureDefinition/BeModelDosagingInformation Version: 1.1.0
Draft as of 2025-04-09 Computable Name: BeModelDosagingInformation

Usage instructions. Instructions about requested administration of the product. Based on FHIR Dosage complex data type.
