ValueSet: Route of Administration ValueSet

Official URL: https://www.ehealth.fgov.be/standards/fhir/terminology/ValueSet/BeRouteOfAdministrationVS Version: 1.1.0
Active as of 2025-09-26 Computable Name: BeRouteOfAdministrationVS

Copyright/Legal: This value set includes content from SNOMED CT, which is copyright Â© 2002+ International Health Terminology Standards Development Organisation (IHTSDO); Belgium is a member of IHTSDO. Implementers of SNOMED CT should review usage terms or directly contact SNOMED International: info@snomed.org

This ValueSet contains codes for the route of administration of medications. It is used to specify how a medication is administered to a patient, such as orally, intravenously, or topically.
